ADA Analytics

During my time at ADA Analytics from December to April, I had the exciting opportunity to work on the backend development team.
Our main project was an ambitious overhaul of the company's website, aiming to revamp it based on new UX/UI prototypes we meticulously designed.

My contributions were centered around developing robust backend functionalities using FastAPI. I created efficient backend endpoints and ensured their reliability through comprehensive unit tests using pytest.
Additionally, I documented all endpoint APIs and utilized Pydantic models for precise response types, ensuring our system's integrity and scalability.
This experience not only honed my technical skills but also deepened my understanding of integrating backend solutions with modern web design principles.
It was a highly rewarding and intellectually stimulating experience that enhanced my expertise in backend development and web technologies.
